üìñ Project Overview

ECOSAGE is an AI-powered environmental intelligence platform combining cutting-edge web technologies with real-time environmental data to promote sustainability awareness and climate action. Built as a full-stack application, it serves as an educational hub for environmental science, biodiversity conservation, and climate change mitigation.
